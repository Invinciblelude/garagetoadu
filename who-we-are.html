Our story

Home Riser Fix exists to make dependable home services—and housing improvements—more accessible. We believe quality work, honest communication, and respectful job sites should be the standard.

We’re also community focused: we work with youth, training the next generation in craftsmanship and responsibility so they can build real skills, earn with dignity, and help strengthen the neighborhoods we serve.

Our long-term goal is simple: help make home services and housing costs more affordable for everyone by building trust, investing in people, and delivering consistent results.
